59. Find Day of the Date in 2016

Write a PHP program to that reads a date (from 2016/1/1 to 2016/12/31) and prints the day of the date. Jan. 1, 2016, is Friday. Note that 2016 is a leap year.

Two integers m and d separated by a single space in a line, m ,d represent the month and the day.
Sample Input:
9 15
Sample Output:

The day is: Thursday
